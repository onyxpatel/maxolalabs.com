ShoreCast
Privacy Policy

ShoreCast is a surf, skim, and beach forecast app. Here is everything about how it handles your data.

Last updated: April 27, 2026 · Maxola Labs LLC · Tampa, FL

Overview

ShoreCast is a forecast app for surfers, skimboarders, and beach-goers. It shows tide windows, swell direction, wind, and spot conditions for coastal locations. The app is built with your privacy in mind: forecast data is fetched from public marine data sources, and your preferences stay on your device.

What ShoreCast Collects

ShoreCast stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your favorite spots and beaches
  • Display preferences (units, theme, layout)
  • Notification preferences for swell and wind alerts

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to Maxola Labs or any third party.

Location Data

ShoreCast may request your location to show forecasts for the nearest spot. Location is used in-session only — it is never stored, logged, or transmitted to any third party. You may use ShoreCast fully without granting location access by manually selecting spots.

Forecast Data Sources

ShoreCast retrieves wave, wind, and tide data from public marine forecast services (such as NOAA and equivalent open data providers). When the app fetches a forecast, only the spot identifier is sent — no personal information, device identifier, or account information is included in these requests.

Sign in with Apple

ShoreCast offers optional Sign in with Apple for syncing favorite spots and preferences across devices. If you choose to use Sign in with Apple, Apple manages your authentication. Maxola Labs receives only the minimal identifier Apple provides and does not receive your email address unless you choose to share it. You may use ShoreCast fully without signing in.

No Advertising or Analytics

ShoreCast contains no advertising networks, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no behavioral tracking. There are no ads in the app.

No Third-Party SDKs

The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks. No third-party code is included.

Children's Privacy

ShoreCast is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No personal data is collected by the app regardless of user age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
