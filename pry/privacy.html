Pry: Security Auditor
Privacy Policy

Your privacy is the foundation of Pry. Here is exactly what we do and do not collect.

Last updated: March 15, 2026 · Maxola Labs LLC · Tampa, FL

Overview

Pry: Security Auditor is designed from the ground up to protect your privacy. All scanning, analysis, and security intelligence runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data or scan results.

What Pry Does Not Collect

Pry does not collect any of the following:

  • Network scan results, device lists, or IP addresses discovered during scans
  • Room scan data, magnetometer readings, or camera footage
  • Bluetooth scan results or detected tracker information
  • Email addresses entered into the breach check feature
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics data
  • Usage patterns, crash logs sent to third parties, or behavioral data

On-Device Processing

Every feature in Pry processes data exclusively on your iPhone. Network scanning uses standard TCP connection probing via Apple's Network framework. Room scanning uses your device's built-in magnetometer and camera. All vulnerability analysis is performed against a bundled local database with no internet connection required.

Breach Check Feature

The breach check feature uses the Have I Been Pwned (HIBP) k-anonymity API. When you enter an email address, Pry computes a SHA-1 hash of the address, sends only the first 5 characters of that hash to the HIBP API, and processes the response entirely on your device. Your email address is never transmitted in full, and HIBP never receives enough information to identify the address being checked. You can verify this independently at haveibeenpwned.com/API/v3.

In-App Purchases

Pry Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Maxola Labs never receives your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Subscription status is verified on-device using Apple's cryptographic transaction verification.

No Account Required

Pry requires no account, no registration, and no login. There is no Maxola Labs server that your app communicates with during normal operation.

No Third-Party SDKs

Pry contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash reporting services, or tracking libraries. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

Data Export

If you use the Pro CSV export feature, the exported file is generated entirely on your device and shared via iOS's native share sheet. The file goes directly to your chosen destination — your Files app, email, AirDrop, etc. Maxola Labs never receives a copy of any exported data.

Children's Privacy

Pry is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not contain features designed for children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Pry after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
