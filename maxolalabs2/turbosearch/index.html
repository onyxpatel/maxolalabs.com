Features

Engineered for Replay Value.

Race the Clock

Every pack is timed. Finish fast for up to 300 bonus points on top of your word score.

Combo Multiplier

Find words back-to-back to stack your combo. Each consecutive find multiplies your score.

Coin-Powered Hints

Earn coins by finding words. Spend them on hints when you're stuck — reveals the first and last letter of the next word.

4 Premium Themes

McLaren Orange, Midnight Racer, Carbon Fiber, Tesla Blue. Every theme is fully implemented — not just a color swap.

Trivia Facts

Every pack ships with a real automotive fact surfaced at completion. Educational as well as entertaining.

Full Accessibility

VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion all supported from day one. Tap mode for VoiceOver users instead of drag.
