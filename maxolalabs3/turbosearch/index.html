Features

Engineered for replay value.

⏱

Race the Clock

Every pack is timed. Finish fast for up to 300 bonus points on top of your word score.

⚡

Combo Multiplier

Find words back-to-back to stack your combo. Each consecutive find multiplies your score.

💡

Coin-Powered Hints

Earn coins by finding words. Spend them to reveal the first and last letter of the next word.

🎨

4 Premium Themes

McLaren Orange, Midnight Racer, Carbon Fiber, Tesla Blue. Fully implemented, not just a color swap.

📖

Trivia Facts

Every pack ships with a real automotive fact surfaced on completion. Educational and entertaining.

♿

Full Accessibility

VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion all supported from day one — including tap mode for VoiceOver users.
