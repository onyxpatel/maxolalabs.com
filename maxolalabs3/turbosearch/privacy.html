Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

MAXOLA LABS LLC built TurboSearch as a commercial iOS application. This policy explains our approach to your data. The short version: we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you — ever.

Information We Do Not Collect

TurboSearch is a word search game. It has no reason to know who you are, and it doesn't try to find out. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information of any kind (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics, behavioral tracking, or gameplay telemetry
  • Any data that requires an account or login to produce

There is no analytics SDK, no third-party tracking library, and no telemetry framework in TurboSearch.

Local Data Storage

TurboSearch stores game progress and preferences locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults and local storage systems. This may include:

  • Pack completion status and star ratings
  • Best times and high scores per pack
  • Coin balance earned through gameplay
  • Selected theme (McLaren Orange, Midnight Racer, Carbon Fiber, Tesla Blue)
  • Accessibility settings (Reduce Motion, Dynamic Type preference)

All of this data exists exclusively on your device. It is never transmitted, never accessible to us, and never shared with any third party. Deleting the app permanently removes it.

No account required. TurboSearch requires no sign-in, no registration, and no personal information to play. All features are available from the moment you open the app.

Sign In with Apple (Optional)

TurboSearch may offer optional Sign In with Apple for future features such as cross-device progress sync. If and when this is implemented:

  • Apple handles all authentication — we receive only an anonymous unique identifier
  • We never receive your name, email address, or Apple ID credentials
  • You may use Apple's "Hide My Email" option to keep your address private
  • Sign in will remain entirely optional — gameplay is never gated behind authentication

Third-Party Services

TurboSearch is a self-contained game with no external data dependencies during gameplay. All word grids, pack content, and trivia facts are bundled within the app itself — no network requests are made to serve game content.

The only potential network activity is Apple's standard iOS system services (e.g., iCloud backup of UserDefaults, if enabled by you in your device settings). This is governed entirely by Apple's privacy policy and your own iOS settings — not by us.

Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to secure on our servers. Your local game progress is protected by your device's built-in security — device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID — managed entirely by Apple's iOS platform.

Children's Privacy

TurboSearch is an automotive word search game accessible to all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, the app is safe for all audiences by design.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
