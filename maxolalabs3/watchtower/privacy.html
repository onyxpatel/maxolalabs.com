Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

MAXOLA LABS LLC built WatchTower as a commercial iOS application. This policy explains our approach to your data. The short version: we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you — ever.

Information We Do Not Collect

WatchTower is built from the ground up with your privacy as a default — not an afterthought. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information of any kind (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics, behavioral tracking, or crash reports sent to us
  • Financial information, portfolio data, or investment preferences
  • Any data whatsoever that requires an account or login to produce

There is no analytics SDK, no third-party tracking library, and no telemetry framework in WatchTower.

Local Data Storage

WatchTower stores a small set of your preferences locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. This may include:

  • Appearance preference (light, dark, or system)
  • Haptic feedback setting (on/off)
  • Onboarding completion state

This data exists exclusively on your device. It is never transmitted, never accessible to us, and never shared with any third party. Deleting the app permanently removes it.

Sign In with Apple

WatchTower offers optional Sign In with Apple for users who want to save preferences across devices. If you choose to use this feature:

  • Apple handles all authentication — we receive only a unique anonymous identifier from Apple
  • We never receive your name, email address, or any Apple ID credentials
  • You may use the "Hide My Email" option to keep your address private from us entirely
  • Sign in is entirely optional — all core features are available without any account

No account required. Every feature in WatchTower is accessible without creating an account or signing in. Authentication is purely optional convenience.

Third-Party Data Services

WatchTower integrates with the following external services to deliver market data and charting. Your device makes direct requests to these services — we do not proxy or log these requests:

  • Yahoo Finance API — Fetches real-time market index and price data. See Yahoo's Privacy Policy.
  • TradingView Widget — Renders interactive charts via an embedded web view. See TradingView's Privacy Policy.
  • SEC EDGAR (via Maxola Labs Cloudflare Worker) — Public SEC 13F filing data is served through our Cloudflare Worker cache. No personal data is passed in these requests — they are anonymous public data fetches.

We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services. We recommend reviewing their individual policies.

Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to secure on our servers. Your local device preferences are protected by your device's built-in security — device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID — managed entirely by Apple's iOS platform.

Children's Privacy

WatchTower is a financial information application intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. As we collect no personal data at all, no special handling is required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
