TurboSearch
Privacy Policy

TurboSearch is a simple word search game. Here is everything about how it handles your data.

Last updated: March 15, 2026 · Maxola Labs LLC · Tampa, FL

Overview

TurboSearch is an automotive word search game built with your privacy in mind. The app stores game progress locally on your device only. We do not collect personal data, and the app functions fully without an internet connection.

What TurboSearch Collects

TurboSearch stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and completed puzzles
  • Selected difficulty level and theme preferences
  • High scores and completion times

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to Maxola Labs or any third party.

Sign in with Apple

TurboSearch offers optional Sign in with Apple for saving game progress across devices. If you choose to use Sign in with Apple, Apple manages your authentication. Maxola Labs receives only the minimal identifier Apple provides and does not receive your email address unless you choose to share it. You may use TurboSearch fully without signing in.

No Advertising or Analytics

TurboSearch contains no advertising networks, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no behavioral tracking. There are no ads in the app.

No Third-Party SDKs

The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks. No third-party code is included.

Children's Privacy

TurboSearch is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No personal data is collected by the app regardless of user age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
